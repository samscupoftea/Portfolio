This is my game project for my application to FAC. In this game, you'll have to catch falling eggs using a virtual basket controlled by the left and right arrow keys.

About Me

Hello there! I'm Sam Hill, and here's a glimpse into my journey. I've worn many hats throughout my life, from exploring the realms of philosophy to venturing into education, local government, and even the vibrant world of music. Along the way, I've gained experiences that have shaped me into who I am today. Let me share a little about my adventures, from teaching in far-flung destinations to creating music and venturing into the world of software development. Join me on this exciting ride!

Multifaceted Background

With a diverse background, I've worked in fields such as education, local government, and music, and I've also taught English abroad. These experiences have given me a unique perspective and skill set.

Musical Talents and Achievements

As a self-taught musician and producer, I'm proud to have achieved international recognition for my music. Some of my songs have been featured on BBC6 Music Radio, and I've had the privilege of performing at renowned venues across the UK.

Teaching and Adaptability

My journey as an English teacher in different countries, including Cambodia, Thailand, and South Korea, has been a testament to my adaptability and passion for education. I even weathered the challenges of the Covid-19 pandemic while continuing to teach effectively.

Passion for Problem Solving

My academic background in Philosophy has instilled in me a deep appreciation for problem-solving. I enjoy tackling a wide range of challenges, from the philosophical to the practical.

Software Development Enthusiasm

I've chosen to pursue software development because I'm driven by my love of learning, creativity, and my passion for technology. My coding skills are showcased through projects like the FAC Mindfulness Generator and the Egg In Basket Game, which reflect my commitment to continuous learning and development in the tech world.
